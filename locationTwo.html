Coptic Church of St.George and St.Shenouda


As one of the most historic places for the Coptic Christian Orthodox diaspora, Jersey City is home to the largest concentration of Coptic people in the world

This Coptic church is the second Coptic church ever built in the Western Hempisphere

This church is a great place to learn about the incredible ethnic and religious diversity of Jersey City and a great place that you can bring your whole family!
