About My Mission


My name is Gabriel Seedhom and I am a student at the New Jersey Institute of Technology and I myself was born in Christ Hospital in Jersey City. During this time of racial turmoil in this country, I have found my home city to be an example of what we can all be as Americans. In Jersey City, the most diverse city in the country, you can find a person of nearly every nationality, race, religion, sexual orientation, and wealth class. As a Jersey City native I can firmly say that we are not at war, but stand with one another. Nowhere else in the world can you find Christians, Muslims, Jews, Hindus, Jehova's Witness, athiests, rich and poor, black and white, hispanic and arab, coming together and living in harmony and brotherly love! My mission is to show off Jersey City in all its beauty and as an example of what the USA can be as a whole! Welcome to Jersey City!!
